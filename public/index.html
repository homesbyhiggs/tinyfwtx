FAQ

How is pricing set?

Lots are leased at market rates and vary with tiny home size and site type (small, large, premium).

Who pays utilities?

Residents pay for their own utilities. Community internet and shared-area utilities are provided by the park.

Can I bring my own tiny home?

Yes—sites are designed for park-model or tiny homes within community guidelines. Final standards will be published before opening.
