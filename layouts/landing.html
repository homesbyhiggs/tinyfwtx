Why I’m Building This Park

In 2012, my mom and I built her a tiny home after we lost ours. I learned two things: housing should be simple, and people deserve certainty. FAQ

How is pricing set?

Lots are leased at market rates and vary with tiny home size and site type (small, large, premium).

Who pays utilities?

Residents pay for their own utilities. Community internet and shared-area utilities are provided by the park.

Can I bring my own tiny home?

Yes—sites are designed for RVIA-certified and Park Model tiny homes. Final standards, including aesthetic requirements, will be published before opening.
