Why I’m Building This Park

In 2012 my mom lost our family home as a result of the 2008 financial crisis. She found stability in a tiny home, and I saw firsthand how lower housing costs create more certainty. I purchased the land where her home now sits and put in much of the work myself. That experience sparked a passion—I went on to build additional tiny homes, which grew into a small micro-resort. With 1,000+ nights hosted and 150+ five-star reviews, I’ve learned what truly makes tiny-home living affordable and sustainable in Fort Worth.

This park will exist to remove barriers for RVIA-certified tiny homes: utility-ready pads, clear rules, vetted transport/setup, and fast move-ins. You get predictable costs, transparent standards, and a local owner who’s been on both sides—resident and operator.

FAQ

How is pricing set?

Lots are leased at market rates and vary with tiny home size and site type (small, large, premium).

Who pays utilities?

Residents pay for their own utilities. Community internet and shared-area utilities are provided by the park.

Can I bring my own tiny home?

Yes—sites are designed for RVIA-certified tiny homes. Final standards, including aesthetic requirements, will be published before opening.
